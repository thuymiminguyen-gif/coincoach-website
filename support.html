Common questions

Frequently asked

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed by Apple, not by CoinCoach. To cancel:

  • Open the Settings app on your iPhone
  • Tap your name at the top → Subscriptions
  • Find CoinCoach Pro and tap it
  • Tap Cancel Subscription

Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period. After that, you'll automatically move to the free tier — your data stays intact.

How do I delete my account?

You can permanently delete your account directly from within the app:

  • Open CoinCoach
  • Tap the Settings icon (top right)
  • Scroll to the Danger Zone section
  • Tap Delete My Account and confirm

Account deletion is permanent and immediate. All your financial data, AI Coach conversations, goals, and personal information are removed from our servers and cannot be recovered.

Does CoinCoach connect to my bank?

No. CoinCoach never connects to your bank, never asks for your bank credentials, and never sees your account numbers. You enter your expenses and income manually, or scan receipts with your camera. Your banking data stays at your bank.

This is intentional. We believe your financial data should stay with you, not be shared with a third-party aggregator.

How does the AI Coach work?

When you ask the AI Coach a question, a summary of your financial data (totals, categories, budgets, and recent transactions) is sent to Anthropic's Claude API to generate a personalized response. Your email address and personal identifiers are not sent.

The AI is grounded in your actual numbers — not generic advice. It can help you understand your spending, plan for goals, and answer questions about your money.

For full technical details, see our Privacy Policy.

What if the AI Coach gives wrong advice?

The AI Coach provides general educational financial guidance. It is not professional financial, tax, legal, or investment advice. Like any AI tool, it can occasionally produce inaccurate or outdated information.

For decisions involving investments, taxes, mortgages, or other major financial decisions, please consult a licensed professional such as a CPA, fiduciary financial advisor, or attorney.

If you notice an error in something the Coach said, we'd love to know — email support@coincoach.app and we'll review it.

How is my data protected?

Your data is stored on Google Firebase using industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. Access is restricted by Firebase Security Rules so that only you can read or modify your own data.

We never sell your data. We never share it with third parties for marketing. We don't show ads.

You can export your data anytime (Dashboard → Export CSV), or permanently delete your account from Settings.

I'm being charged but the app isn't working — what do I do?

First, try these steps:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it
  • Make sure you're signed in to the same account where you purchased
  • In Settings, tap Restore Purchases

If you're still having trouble, email us at support@coincoach.app with your Apple receipt (Apple emails this to you after purchase) and a description of the issue. We respond within 1–2 business days.

How do I request a refund?

Subscription refunds are handled by Apple, not by CoinCoach. To request a refund:

  • Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  • Sign in with your Apple ID
  • Find your CoinCoach subscription
  • Tap Report a Problem and follow the prompts

Apple decides refund eligibility on a case-by-case basis. If they decline, feel free to email us at support@coincoach.app — we'll do what we can to help.

Will CoinCoach be available on Android or web?

We're launching on iOS first to build the product carefully with a focused audience. Android and a full web version are on our roadmap, but we don't have a timeline yet.

If you'd like to be notified when these become available, join the waitlist on the home page.

Is CoinCoach suitable for minors?

CoinCoach is rated 4+ on the App Store, but the financial planning features are designed for adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact privacy@coincoach.app and we'll delete it.
